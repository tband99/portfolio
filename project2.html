CubeSat ADCS Development (NDI & MEKF) (In Progress)

As part of my senior capstone team, I’m working on the Attitude Determination and Control System (ADCS) for a CubeSat imaging mission. Our goal is to stabilize and point the satellite accurately for image capture. I’m helping design and simulate control algorithms using Nonlinear Dynamic Inversion (NDI) and a Multiplicative Extended Kalman Filter (MEKF) in MATLAB. We’re currently testing these models in simulation, with plans to move into hardware testing once they meet performance targets.

Highlights

  • NDI controller implementation in MATLAB
  • MEKF state estimation for attitude pointing
  • Preparing for hardware-in-the-loop testing
