Autonomous Drone Build (ROS, Gazebo, Ardupilot Integration) (In Progress)

I’m building a custom quadrotor to learn how to develop fully autonomous drones from the ground up. The project combines simulation and real-world testing using ROS, Gazebo, and Ardupilot. My focus is on flight control, sensor integration, and obstacle avoidance — creating a system that can perceive its surroundings and make intelligent navigation decisions. This project is helping me strengthen my understanding of autonomy frameworks, control loops, and the challenges of bridging simulation and hardware.

Highlights

  • Simulation with Gazebo and ROS integration
  • Interfacing flight controller with Ardupilot
  • Developing obstacle avoidance and mapping pipelines
