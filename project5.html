Carnival Game (STEM Outreach Project)

Developed a Hall effect sensor system integrated with Arduino and IoT to track scoring in a water cannon game. Deployed real-time scoreboard used by 400+ participants during a STEM event.

Highlights

  • Arduino + IoT integration for real-time scoring
  • Sensor calibration and robustness testing
  • Designed for outreach and large-event reliability
